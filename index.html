Crystal Collector

How to play...


A random number is created at the start of each game.

The four crystals below, add a specifc number to the your total score when clicked.

To win, simply match your total score with the random number. You will lose if your total score exceeds the random number.

Becareful! The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

At the beginning of each game the crystals value will change.
